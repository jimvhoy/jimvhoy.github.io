About This Activity

This activity is about creating an E-Portfolio, which serves as an online collection of my works, skills, and experiences as a IT student. The main purpose of this task is to help me build a professional digital portfolio that showcases who I am as a future Software Engineer or Web Developer.

Through this project, I will apply my knowledge in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design an interactive and user-friendly website that reflects good UI/UX design principles. The portfolio will include my resume, introduction, projects, and work experiences—allowing others such as employers, teachers, or clients to easily see my qualifications and accomplishments.

Overall, this activity aims to prepare me for the professional world by helping me create an online platform that represents my identity, technical skills, and growth in the IT field.
